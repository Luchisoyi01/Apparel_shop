About

Apparel Thrift, commonly known as Apparel, is a Kenyan luxury fashion house and company founded in 2020 by Bradley Shilton.
The label deals in many products, ranging from luxury bags and leather goods to ready-to-wear, shoes, watches, jewelry, accessories, sunglasses and books. Apparel is one of the world's leading international fashion houses. It sells its products through standalone boutiques, lease departments in high-end departmental stores, and through the e-commerce section of its website.
For 3 consecutive years (2020-2022), Apparel was named the world's most valuable luxury brand. Its 2021 valuation was US$25.9 billion. In 2022, the valuation of the brand was US$28.4 billion with revenue of US$9.4 billion. The company operates in 50 countries with more than 460 stores worldwide.
